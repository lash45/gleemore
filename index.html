Engaging the Future Now...

Who we are

We are an educational agency with an unequivocal aim to be a foremost educational and inspirational outfit for the young minds in schools, helping them shape their future thereby adding values to society through proper youth development. To make schooling more lively and purposeful by exploring issues pertaining to students’ desires and challenges as well as engaging them in learning-enhancing programs and activities

What we do

Educational Trainings

Our major engagement at Gleemore Consult is to facilitate students at both private and public institutions on various educational programs and activities which include among others career orientation/development, writing seminars and exploratory research that ensures purposeful and lively learning process.

Writing Seminars

At Gleemore Consult, we strongly believe that writing, in whatever form, sharpens and open up analytical abilities resident in the human brain thereby improving critical thinking and increasing one’s desires for reading. As we know, there are no good writers without good readers. Thus for students to succeed in their academic endeavours at any level, learning to writing excellently is inevitable. As a result, we render expert guideance to those interested in taking writing as a career and gradually determine what kind of writing bests suites the personality of such student. Ultimately, we intend to motivate young minds to explore writing as an enjoyable mental exercise and a medium of intellectual and academic enlightenment.

Entrepreneural Development

We focus on young minds with strong entrepreneurial drive and interests. In order to foster the entrepreneurial spirit, we come up with several initiatives and incentives for such courses with the goal of recaliberating the perception about entrepreneurship created by an educational system that leaves a lot to be desired. However, it was found out that the school related initiatives alone have not been able to yield expected results in preparing talented minds for the job market , hence, our goal is to bridge this gap by improving employability and productivity each mind.

Gender Awareness

In addition to our educational services, GM Consult also educates students on the need to be sensitive to their gender and keep themselves abreast of its social configurations in order to rise above the ensuing challenges as they progress in life. We sensitize young girls and boys on etiquettes and behavioural expectations of responsible young adults inorder to foster healthy interaction with the opposite sex as they grow older.

Editorial Services

Our editorial services include copy editing and proofreading which cover basic errors in spelling, punctuation, grammar, formatting, italicisation, hyphenation and overall improvement of the quality of text. We also engage in ghostwriting, that is, writing for people who commission us to write on any topics for them. Such topics may include among others, autobiographies, public lectures. curriculum vitae and the likes.
